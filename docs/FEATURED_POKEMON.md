## Design Principles

1. **Legendarity:** Each Pok√©mon is either legendary, mythical, or a strong metagame figure
2. **Regional Representation:** Each generation showcases its core legendaries
3. **Variety:** No overuse of individual Pok√©mon (especially important: Charizard appears in only 3 PDFs, not all)
4. **Thematic Coherence:** Sections have thematically fitting Pok√©mon
5. **Symbolism:** Triads represent forces or concepts (Time-Space-Distortion, Legendary Birds, etc.)
